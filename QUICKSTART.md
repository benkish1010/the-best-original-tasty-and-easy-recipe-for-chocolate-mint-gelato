# Quick Start Guide - Chocolate Mint Gelato

**Want to make gelato right now?** Here's the condensed version!

## What You Need

### Ingredients
- 600g whole milk
- 200g heavy cream
- 150g sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 30g fresh mint leaves
- 40g cocoa powder
- 100g dark chocolate (60-70%)
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Equipment
- Ice cream maker (bowl frozen 24+ hours)
- Thermometer
- Pot, whisk, strainer
