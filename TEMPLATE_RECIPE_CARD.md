- [ ] 600g whole milk

### QUICK STEPS

#### 1. MINT INFUSION (15 min)
- Heat milk + cream + mint to steaming (75-80¬∞C)
- Don't boil! Cover and steep 10 min
- Strain out mint (press gently)

#### 2. ADD CHOCOLATE (5 min)
- Whisk in sugar + cocoa + salt
- Add chocolate, stir until melted (60-70¬∞C)

#### 3. COOK CUSTARD (12 min) ‚ö†Ô∏è
- Whisk egg yolks in bowl
- Slowly add 3 portions of hot mixture (tempering)
- Return to pot, cook to **82-84¬∞C**
- Stir constantly! Should coat spoon

#### 4. CHILL (12+ hours)
- Ice bath 30 min
- Refrigerate overnight
- Must be <4¬∞C

#### 5. CHURN (25 min)
- Pour into ice cream maker
- Churn 15-30 min (soft-serve texture)
- Add chips in last 2 minutes

#### 6. FREEZE (3 hours)
- Transfer to container
- Freeze 2-4 hours
- Let sit 5-10 min before serving
