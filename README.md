# The Best Original Tasty and Easy Recipe for Gelato

This guide explains how to make authentic Italian gelato at home
using simple ingredients and easy steps.

## Ingredients
- Milk
- Cream
- Sugar
- Egg yolks
- Vanilla

## Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Heat the milk and cream
2. Whisk egg yolks and sugar
3. Temper the eggs
4. Chill the mixture
5. Churn in an ice cream maker

## Tips for Perfect Gelato
- Lower fat than ice cream
- Slow churning
- Serve slightly warmer
